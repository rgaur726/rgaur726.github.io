Hello! I Am

Rishabh Gaur

I currently work as an Account Technology Strategist for Microsoft's GSMO division, focusing on product & strategy aspects of Azure. Before that, I've spent five years in Pilani, getting a dual degree from Birla Institute of Technology and Science. During my undergrad, I've completed summer internships at MNCs like Microsoft and Abbott, startups like Zimetrics and I also did a research intern at CSIR-CEERI. Outside of work, I enjoy playing cricket, surfing reddit and occasionally writing an odd post here and there. I also write at Evolution One , focusing on AI & machine learning.
