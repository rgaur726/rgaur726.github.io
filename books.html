Hi there! I'm Rishabh. I currently work for Microsoft's in their GSMO division, focusing on product & strategy aspects of Azure. Before that, I've completed my education from BITS Pilani, getting a dual degree from the class of 2019, with M.Sc. (Hons.) in Biological Sciences and B.E. (Hons.) in Mechanical Engineering.

Previously, I've interned at MNCs like Microsoft where I worked on NLP and deep learning, and Abbott Laboratories where I worked as a software development intern, and at a start-up Zimetrics where I worked on multiple projects dealing with everthing from the medical sector to the Indian Navy, while I also did a research intern at CSIR-CEERI where we worked on dealing with cervical cancer using artificial intelligence.

Outside of work, I enjoy playing cricket (typical Indian), I love reading and occasionally writing an odd post here and there. I also write at Evolution One, focusing on AI & machine learning.

You can always find me online at Facebook, LinkedIn, Reddit or e-mail me.
